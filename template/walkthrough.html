You can extract your gene set from the MA-plot and/or Volcano-plot in 3 steps.
  1. Load your DESeq2 results table (differential analysis)
  2. Select parameter cutoffs with the sliders (tab 'Parameters' on the left), type in your gene (text panel above MA-plot) or draw frames in one of the plots - the other plot will react
  3. Switch to tab 'Table - selected data' to view, search and download your selected gene set

Further, the 'Statistics' panel on the left side, shows you the number of differential genes according to the selected slider cutoffs.

A speciality of the plots is, that they show you
  1. tool tips with information to each gene (feature)
  2. you can zoom
  3. and something else

The tabs 'Table - preview input' and 'Sessioninfo' are for debug purpose only
